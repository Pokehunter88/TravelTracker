# 3. JE : Jersey pound (1:1 pegged to the pound sterling)			
# 4. IM : Isle of Man pound (1:1 pegged to the pound sterling)																		
# 5. TV : Tuvaluan dollar (1:1 pegged to the Australian dollar).
